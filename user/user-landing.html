BARANGAY VITO COMPLAINT AND INCIDENT REPORTING SYSTEM

About iReklamo

The Barangay Vito Complaint and Incident Reporting System (iReklamo) serves as an online system for reporting and managing complaints and incidents within the community. It is created to make it easier for residents to reach out to barangay officials and for officials to respond in a timely and organized manner.

Through iReklamo, residents can submit reports such as noise disturbances, sanitation issues, disputes, or other incidents. We pledge to address every concern with fairness, respond to reports promptly, and take necessary action to maintain the safety and welfare of the community.
